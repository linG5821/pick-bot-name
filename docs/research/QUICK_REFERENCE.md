## Pattern Rules

### English
- **Length:** 3-12 characters
- **Format:** Single word > [Function]bot > Compound
- **Avoid:** "AI", "Smart", "Intelligent" prefixes
- **Best:** 1-2 syllable single words (Fin, Ada, Drift)

### Chinese
- **Length:** 2-3 characters (90% of real bots)
- **Maximum:** 4 characters
- **NEVER:** 5+ characters or "的" constructions
- **Best:** 小X (2 chars) or X君 (3 chars)

### Priority by Context

**English Professional:**
1. Single word (Fin, Guide, Scout)
2. [Function]bot (Workbot, Taskbot)
3. Simple compound (max 2 words)

**Chinese Professional:**
1. X君 (助理君, 文档君) - 3 chars
2. X助手 (审批助手, 会议助手) - 3-4 chars
3. 小X (小智, 小助) - 2 chars

**Chinese Friendly:**
1. 小X (小智, 小飞) - 2 chars
2. Reduplication (喵喵, 团团) - 2 chars

## Anti-Patterns (Don't Do This)

### English
❌ BusinessAssistantPro (20 chars - too long)
❌ SmartAIHelper (generic, overused)
❌ IntelligentWorkflowBot (too wordy)

### Chinese
❌ 可爱的小猫助手 (7 chars - has "的", too long)
❌ 智能机器人助手 (6 chars - redundant)
❌ 企业办公协作助手机器人 (11 chars - way too long)
